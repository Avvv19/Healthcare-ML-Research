## Overview

A research repository exploring the application of **Machine Learning** techniques to solve real-world healthcare challenges. This collection covers predictive modeling, clinical data analysis, and AI-driven insights that can improve patient outcomes, operational efficiency, and medical decision-making across multiple healthcare domains.

---

## Research Areas

### Clinical Outcome Prediction
- Hospital readmission prediction
- Patient risk stratification
- Length-of-stay forecasting

### Medical Image Analysis
- Diagnostic classification models
- Anomaly detection in medical imaging
- Computer-aided diagnosis systems

### Drug Discovery & Genomics
- Molecular property prediction
- Gene expression analysis
- Treatment response modeling

### Population Health Analytics
- Epidemiological trend analysis
- Public health intervention modeling
- Health disparities research
